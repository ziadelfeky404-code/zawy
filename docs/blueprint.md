## Style Guidelines:

- Primary brand color: A clean, trustworthy dark teal (#448D92) to reflect professionalism and innovation, inspired by the logo.
- Background color: An extremely light, cool off-white (#F9FCFC) to provide a spacious, minimal canvas, subtly harmonizing with the primary teal hue.
- Accent color: A soft, muted greenish-blue (#8BC3B2) that serves as an analogous highlight, adding subtle contrast and visual interest to interactive elements without being overly bold.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a modern sans-serif typeface, selected for its neutrality, excellent legibility, and professional appearance across various content types, suitable for both headlines and extended body text in Arabic RTL.
- Use a consistent style of line-art or subtle filled illustrations for icons, specifically representing themes of education, accessibility, Braille, and assistive technology, to maintain a modern and professional aesthetic.
- Implement an Arabic-first, Right-to-Left (RTL) responsive layout with a clean grid system. Employ generous whitespace around sections and content blocks to ensure readability and convey a premium, minimal startup feel across all devices.
- Incorporate soft, subtle animations such as gentle fades on section visibility, smooth hover effects on interactive elements like cards and buttons, and fluid transitions to enhance user engagement without distraction.
